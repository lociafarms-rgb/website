About Locia Farms

Locia Farms is a family-owned farm dedicated to providing fresh, quality products to our community. We take pride in raising our animals with care and growing produce using sustainable farming practices.

Our commitment to quality and sustainability means you can trust that every product from Locia Farms meets our high standards.

We believe in connecting our community with where their food comes from. Visit us at the farm to see our operations firsthand and meet the animals that make it all possible.
